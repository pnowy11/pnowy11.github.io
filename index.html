Welcome to the Johnno's unofficial yet official website!

Welcome to Johnno's Mini Market. Johnno's Mini Market is a corner shop

in which you can find a whole range of items, this range only typically found

in larger shops such as co-op, lidl and the such. This is where the name

came from, Johnno's Mini Market because it tries its best to immitate larger

market like shops whilst still providing varying quality products!





The shop is open from 7:00am to 21:00pm from Monday to Friday,

however on Saturday and Sunday it's open from 7:30am to 20:00pm

as the weekends tends to be less busy than week days.

Johnno's Mini Market is located at 117 Sneinton Rd, Nottingham NG2 4QL

and its really compact for the sheer range of items and groceries you can buy!

Johnno's Mini Market makes sure all it's products are safe and fresh

and ready for consumption or use. This means all perishable

products are restocked a few days before they expire. There are also

many high quality branded products fill the mini market's shelves

whilst there are also more off-license high quality products.
